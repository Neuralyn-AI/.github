# Neuralyn - AI Solutions

[Neuralyn](https://www.neuralyn.com.br/) is an innovative AI startup focused on building next-generation intelligent systems that seamlessly integrate artificial intelligence into real-world business experiences. Founded in Brazil, in São Paulo, Neuralyn is headquartered in the Perdizes region—one of the city’s most dynamic technology and innovation hubs.

The company designs modular, scalable AI solutions that combine advanced machine learning, computer vision, and conversational intelligence to transform how people interact with digital products and physical environments. At its core, Neuralyn develops AI-powered platforms for commerce, customer engagement, and immersive experiences—ranging from virtual try-on technology for fashion and retail to intelligent digital concierges and interactive kiosks.

Neuralyn’s solutions are API-first, cloud-native, and built with performance, security, and cost efficiency in mind, allowing businesses to adopt artificial intelligence with minimal friction. By blending cutting-edge research with strong product design and engineering, Neuralyn empowers brands to personalize experiences at scale, optimize operations, and create more human-centric interactions powered by AI.
